I am a staff scientist at Caltech GPS as well as an affiliate at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory
(Click the colored text to view each official profile)

I received my Bachelor of Engineering in Resource Engineering from Hokkaido University in 2016 and my Ph.D. in Physical Chemistry from MIT in 2022.

My research interests and projects including observations are

  • Cross-disciplinary characterization of various exoplanet atmospheres
  • The evolution of Archean Earth's atmosphere and the Great Oxidation Event (GOE)
  • Atmospheric engineering of Martian and Venusian atmosphere
  • Search for habitable worlds and origin of life
  • Fundamental and applied photochemical kinetics of Sulfur species
  • (Co-I) James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) cycle 2 GO 3942 (PI: Dr. Mario Damiano)
  • (Co-I) James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) cycle 3 GO 4711 (PI: Dr. Renyu Hu)
  • (Co-I) James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) cycle 3 GO 5177 (PI: Dr. Renyu Hu)
